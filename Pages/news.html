February 7, 2023: Quick Update

We've rolled out initial versions of two new modules (7 and 8) for the Linear Algebra course. We are also working on setting up a user authentication system so that users may save their progress and submit their results for credit. In the meantime we are continuing our review of current literature in inquiry based learning.

February 2, 2023: Hello World. Here is the first status update from Project Intuition

We have now completed the software foundation on which we plan to construct our product. This includes the structure of the site, the format of the modules, the basic scripts needed for interactivity and assessment along with a SQL database for recording user statistics and a two way data pipeline for recording data to, and retrieving data from, the database.

We have also begun construction on our first lab course which will be Linear Algebra. So far we have completed modules 1-6 of a planned 20-25. We hope to have this initial course done by the beginning of the 2023 spring quarter for universities in the Western U.S. in order to begin collecting data at that point. This data will help us to improve the Linear Algebra course and will inform our construction of future courses.
